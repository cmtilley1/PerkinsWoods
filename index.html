About Perkins

Perkins Woods is a 7.5 acre woodland remnant of "The Big Woods." The dominance, density and frequency of Basswoods and Silver Maples recorded on site during GES 327 quarter point evaluations in 2018 indicate a Wet Forest community (Chicago Wilderness Atlas of Biodiversity, 1999.)

Friends of the Forest Preserve . This link takes you to the Friends of the Forest Preserve webpage to an article written about Siberian squill located within the woods.

Map 1

Through the use of HTML coding and work on QGIS Sam has created a map that allows all of the different species of trees that was inventoried at Perkins to be displayed. We sought to create an interactive map that allows the user to select which species of trees they wish to display by creating a different layer for each species of tree. With this, users can look at the various frequency and densities of different tree species in this area.
